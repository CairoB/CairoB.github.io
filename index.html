My personal interests

My absolute favorite hobby is video games. That's what got me into computers as a kid and it's what inspires me to build, design, and create. I play PC games of all kinds; single-player, multi-player, sandbox, competitive, top-down, third-person... I'll play anything!

I also enjoy tabletop RPGs. The most famous is Dungeons and Dragons of course, but I play other ones, too. A group of friends and I actually have a prototype system of our own, complete with combat mechanics, items, statistics, and a complete world of lore.

I play the guitar, bass, and piano. When I started with music my parents wanted me to play the piano, but I convinced them to get me a guitar instead. I've been playing for 11 years, more than half of my entire life. It turned out I got into the piano after learning in-depth music theory, so I guess my parents got their way after all!
